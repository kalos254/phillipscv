PROJECT IMPACT EVALUATION & QUANTIFICATION


I have lead in designing project evaluation methodologies, designing data collection tools, analysis (evidence generation) and report writing. The evaluation includes baseline, mid-term and end of project assessment or impact evaluation. My focus is on projects dealing with livelihood support, food and nutrition security, resilience to climate shocks, WASH, markets and value chains. I am conversant with OECD-DAC criteria, Results Based Analysis (RBA) and Quantitative Project Impact Evaluation Techniques including Randomization techniques, Difference-in-Difference (D-i-D), Instrumental Variable (IV), Propensity Score Matching (PSM), and Regression.

FOOD SECURITY, NUTRITION AND POVERTY ANALYSIS


I am skilled in food security, nutrition and poverty analysis.. I am highly conversant with quantitative analysis of food security and nutrition indicators including- Food Consumption Scores (FCS), Household Hunger Scales (HHS), Household Dietary Diversity (HDDS), Coping Strategy Index (CSI) and Poverty incidence as well as poverty decomposition measures- Poverty incidence, gap and severity. I have analyzed food security and poverty in several assignments examining food security and poverty drivers to inform programmatic and public policy interventions. AGRICULTURAL VALUE CHAINS ANALYSIS: I have lead as a resercher/consultant in value chain studies covering crops, livestock and Natural resources. Such assignments have entailed development of the overall study methodologies, designing data collection tools, designing the samples, data collection and management of quality, Data analysis (Qualitative and Quantitative), and report writing.
